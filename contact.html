Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can SK Associiates deliver materials?

For local areas like Namakkal, Karur, and nearby districts, we typically deliver within 1–2 working days. For outstation or large bulk orders, delivery time may extend depending on availability and logistics.

Do you assist with product application?

Yes, we provide full technical support including site visits, demonstrations, and product usage guidance for contractors, engineers, and end clients.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

There's no strict minimum for local pickup. For delivery, order quantity depends on the product type and brand. Contact us for specific MOQ details.

Do you offer pricing for bulk or long-term projects?

Absolutely. We offer discounted pricing for bulk purchases and recurring project needs. Builders and developers can request custom quotes.

Which brands do you supply?

We deal with trusted brands like Saint-Gobain Weber, Choksey, Vito, Akemi, and more—ensuring top quality in every category from tile care to waterproofing.

Can you recommend the right product for my project?

Yes. Based on your site conditions and needs, we can suggest suitable construction chemicals and provide usage instructions for optimal results.

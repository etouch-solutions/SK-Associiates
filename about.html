About SK Associiates

SK Associiates is a trusted name in the construction industry, providing advanced chemical solutions for sustainable construction. Affiliated with national organizations like BAI, BNI, VCEA, and WAI, we specialize in tile care, stone care, and waterproofing using premium products like Weber (Saint-Gobain), VITO, and AKEMI. Our focus is high-quality products with technical guidance for both residential and commercial needs in South India.

Our Mission

To empower builders with industry-leading construction chemical solutions and on-site support, ensuring durable and aesthetically superior structures.

Our Vision

To be South India's most trusted construction chemical partner through quality, expertise, and reliable service.

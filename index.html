About SK Associiates

SK Associiates is a trusted name in the construction industry, providing advanced chemical solutions for sustainable construction. Affiliated with national organizations like BAI, BNI, VCEA, and WAI, we specialize in tile care, stone care, and waterproofing using premium products like Weber (Saint-Gobain), VITO, and AKEMI. Our focus is high-quality products with technical guidance for both residential and commercial needs in South India.

Our Mission

To empower builders with industry-leading construction chemical solutions and on-site support, ensuring durable and aesthetically superior structures.

Our Vision

To be South India's most trusted construction chemical partner through quality, expertise, and reliable service.

Quality Products we offer

Tile Care Solutions (Saint-Gobain Weber)

We distribute a complete range of tile adhesives, grouts, and tile cleaners from Saint-Gobain Weber, a global leader in construction solutions. Our tile care products are easy to apply, quick to move, and trusted by masons and dealers across the region.

Stone Care Solutions (Vito, Akemi & More)

We supply professional stone care products suitable for marble, granite, quartz, and vitrified surfaces. Our range includes cleaners, polishers, sealants, and color enhancers that protect and beautify natural stones.

Building Care & Waterproofing

Our product lineup includes advanced waterproofing systems, repair mortars, crack fillers, surface protectors, and admixtures. These are critical for long-lasting buildings, used by civil engineers, contractors, and government projects.
